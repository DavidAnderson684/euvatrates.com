Welcome to the EU VAT Rates Web Service

Born out of the need to have some sort of web service or API to retrieve current VAT Rates to apply the 2015 EU VAT Regulation, this Sunday night hack-a-thon is exactly that.

Do not rely on this in production (or something meaning to go to production). It's super alpha and may be discontinued without warning.

How it works

Simply load http://euvatrates.com/rates.json (Click!) and use the data to apply the proper VAT rates in your cart or checkout system.

Considering these VAT rates rarely change, cache the data on your system. If you repeatedly hit the server and steal up the bandwidth, you risk getting blocked from accessing this free service.

The data structure is pretty self explanatory JSON. There's a last_updated timestamp that tells you when the last VAT change (or bug fix) was made, and there is a disclaimer. The rates object contains all the different rates per country, based on its 2 character key.

The rate object contains the country name, and a couple of different tax rates that exist in the country. The currently returned rate types are standard_rate, reduced_rate, reduced_rate_alt, super_reduced_rate and parking_rate. The values are always numeric (with decimals), or a boolean false if the rate is not used in the country.

Which VAT rate you need to use, depends on the type of product or service you are providing and to whom you are providing it. It is best to consult someone knowledgeable, like your accountant on that. This official EU Document also contains a table indicating what rates to apply. Exceptions or exemptions may apply per country.
